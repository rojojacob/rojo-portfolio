01 · About

Four-plus years of shipping apps that rank #1.

I'm Rojo Jacob, an Associate Team Lead and Senior iOS Developer based in Kochi, Kerala. I spend my days in Xcode turning tight product constraints into native interfaces people actually enjoy touching.

I studied Electronics & Communication Engineering before going all-in on iOS. Today I steer a team of 10+ developers at Webandcrafts, mentoring, reviewing code, authoring time estimations, and owning features end-to-end from architecture to App Store Connect.

If it ships with my name on it, it had better be fast, quiet, and delightful that's the work that won me the Star Performer Award, 2025.
